Risk Analysis - Beta
     

A - Project Management and Governance
Were consideration been put in the current project scope and been well defined? Were sufficient processes been put in place to minimize the lack of accountability when things go wrong? Do the stakeholders correctly represent their roles? Did the project have all the key roles represented? Were schedules been completed and followed? Were the milestones and deadlines realistic and achievable? were there low turnover in leadership and staff positions?

B - Project Development and Training
Were there any code changes or development made during code freeze? Did the stakeholders refrain from making decisions based on optimism? Were end users given adequate training?

C - Implementation / Go-Live
Have all the requirements been completed? Was the implementation strategy reviewed and chosen to be the most appropriate? Are there adequate support for end users after Rollout? Will a phased rollout be followed? Has it been ensured that no risks are carried into the Go-Live?

D - Planning & Requirements
Was appropriate research done for the selection of vendor? Was there enough planning done for the project? Including goals, phases, and deadlines? Were all user requirements been laid out and assessed? Were the negotiations between client and vendor clear and suitable for long term agreement?

E - Project Finance
Was it ensured that the budget was on track? Was the budget properly reviewed by someone with experience in similar projects? Were there processes in place to mitigate potential delays and the associated costs?

F - Communication & Collaboration
Did the team members know what their role and responsibilities were at all project levels? Was there continued presence by the top-level roles in the project? was there transparency between the different stakeholders of the project? Were the end users being involved during the project phases as a form of feedback and training?
