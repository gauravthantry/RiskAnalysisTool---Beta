Risk Analysis - Beta
     

A - Project Management and Governance
Has consideration been put in the current project scope and been well defined? Have sufficient processes been put in place to minimize the lack of accountability when things go wrong? Does the project have a selection of stakeholders which accurately represent the project? Are there any gaps of key roles at any level of the project? Have schedules been completed? Are the milestones and deadlines in place realistic and achievable? Has there been minimal turnover in leadership and staff positions?

B - Project Development and Training
Are there actions in place to ensure no development is continued during a code freeze? Are there processes in place to ensure decisions are not based off optimism? Is there processes/plans to give end users adequate training prior to the go-live?
